About Bloombot

What is Bloombot?

Bloombot is a smart gardening system designed to help users monitor and manage their plants effortlessly. Whether you're an experienced gardener or just getting started, Bloombot provides tools to track plant status, set watering thresholds, and receive alerts when plants need care.

Our Mission

We aim to simplify gardening using smart technology. With real-time monitoring and data-driven alerts, Bloombot ensures your plants stay healthy while saving time and reducing water waste.

Who is it for?

Bloombot is ideal for home gardeners, schools, offices, and plant enthusiasts who want to take better care of their plants with minimal effort.
